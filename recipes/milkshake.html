Chocolate Milkshake

A heavenly chocolate milkshake that is bound to rock your world

This delicious chocolate milkshake recipe will go down well with everyone in the family. From the kids, to the parents, and even that pesky Aunt Gladys won't be able to resist!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Chocolate
  • Milk
  • Colourful Sprinkles

Steps

  1. Chuck all ingredients in a bowl and stir
  2. Pour in a glass and make it look nice for instagram
  3. Let Aunt Gladys try it first
