Spaghetti Bolognese

A Bolognese recipe with real personality

This delicious Bolognese recipe will make your tastebuds sing. With its rich tomato based sauce and special ingredients, this is sure to become a staple recipe in your household.

Ingredients

  • 400g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Brown Onion
  • 500g Minced Beef
  • 100g Mushrooms
  • 1 Carrot
  • 2 Celery Sticks
  • 1 Tin of Anchovies
  • 1 tbsp of Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1 tsp of Sugar
  • 300g Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Chop the ingredients into medium sized chunks
  2. Lob it all in a pan on a medium heat
  3. Cook for a little while
  4. Once thickened up a bit, bring a pan of water to the boil
  5. Lob the Spaghetti in and bring back down to a simmer
  6. After 10 mins, drain the spaghetti and then chuck in with the meat & sauce mixture
  7. There you go! Amazing spag bol!
